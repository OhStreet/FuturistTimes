AI

Artificial Intelligence has seen an ever-increasing boom in advancement, particularly when it comes to generative AI.

Rapid advancement

Over the past few years, we have seen such an increase in quality from these AI models, that we went from generating abstract images based on a prompt, to capturing and animating motion in real time! - https://www.rokoko.com/products/vision

Aside from art, AI has so far been used for many practical tasks. As these tools start to become more frequent in the workplace, they will inevitably reach a point where they are a necessity. Here is an article that highlights ways AI is used today - https://www.mckinsey.com/capabilities/mckinsey-digital/our-insights/the-top-trends-in-tech#new-and-notable

The Singularity

However, due to this tech advancing at a seemingly exponential rate, it is important to be aware of the potential implications. What if we reach a point where AI becomes more intelligent than humans? Will they solve world issues? How will their perspectives on us humans change?

This event is sometimes referred to as the Technological Singularity. Take a look at this article to learn more about the theory - https://builtin.com/artificial-intelligence/technological-singularity.
